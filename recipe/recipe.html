Vegetarian Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz (225g) pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber,diced
  • 1/2 red onion,thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup black olives,sliced
  • 1/4 cup fetamcheese,crumbled
  • 2 tablesoones olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the paste according to package Instructions.Drain and let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl,combine the cooked pasta,cherry tomatoes,cucumber,red onion,olives,and feta cheese.
  3. In a small bowl,whish together the olive oil,balsamic vinegar,salt and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss to combine.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
