Must-Read Books

Fiction

The Great Gatsby

By F. Scott Fitzgerald
A classic novel exploring the American dream and the roaring twenties.

To Kill a Mockingbird

By Harper Lee
An impactful story of racial injustice and moral growth in the American South.

Science Fiction

Dune

By Frank Herbert
An epic science fiction novel set in a distant future amidst a sprawling interstellar empire.

Neuromancer

By William Gibson
A cyberpunk masterpiece that explores the intersection of technology and humanity.
